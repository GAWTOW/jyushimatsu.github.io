Who is Jyushimatsu Matsuno?

Jyushimatsu Matsuno is a character from the 2015 anime, Osomatsu-san. He is the fifth son of the Matsuno family, who is also apart of identical sextuplets. He is voiced by Daisuke Ono, a popular voice actor in Japan.
